Haley Ann Bird has taken the following reasonable measures to maintain the secrecy of the trade secrets listed above:

2. **No Third-Party Disclosure** — No confidential technical details have been shared with any third party without a signed Non-Disclosure Agreement.

3. **Platform Security** — GitHub's access control, two-factor authentication, and repository privacy settings are maintained on all repositories containing trade secret material.

4. **Selective Public Disclosure** — Only the specific elements listed in the "Publicly Disclosed" section above have been intentionally made public. All other details remain confidential.

5. **This Document** — The existence of this formal trade secret designation document itself constitutes a reasonable measure under 18 U.S.C. § 1839(3).
