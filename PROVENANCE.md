## What This Document Does

This Provenance Chain document performs four legal functions simultaneously:

1. **Prior Art Publication** — Under 35 U.S.C. § 102(b)(1), a public disclosure by the inventor establishes prior art against all third parties who later file patent applications on the same subject matter. Every section below is a dated public disclosure.

2. **Reduction to Practice** — Under U.S. patent law, an invention is "reduced to practice" when it is either (a) built and demonstrated to work (actual reduction) or (b) described in a patent application with sufficient detail (constructive reduction). This document provides constructive reduction to practice for all systems listed.

3. **Trade Secret Boundary** — The disclosed portions of each system establish the public boundary. All non-disclosed implementation details retain trade secret protection under DTSA (18 U.S.C. § 1836) as long as they are kept confidential and subject to reasonable security measures (this repository's private mirrors satisfy that requirement).

4. **Copyright Authorship Record** — Under 17 U.S.C. § 102, copyright vests in the author at the moment of creation. Git commit timestamps provide cryptographically ordered evidence of creation date for all text, code, architecture diagrams, and data structures herein.
